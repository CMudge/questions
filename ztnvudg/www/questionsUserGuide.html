User Guide

Welcome to the Question Setting App!

This apps allows you to create questions for use in a location-based quiz game. All you have to do is click on the map to choose the location for your question, and fill in the form with the contents of your question.

You must give four possible answers (of which one must be correct!), and select which one is the correct answer.

When you've finished, submit your question by clicking on the 'Submit Question' button. You will be told if the submission was successful.

If you want to submit another question, simply click on the map again and fill in the form as before with your new question.

The Side Bar

The 'Home' button reloads the page. The 'User Guide' button opens this page.

Trouble Shooting

If you're receiving an error when submitting a question, check to see if you have an apostrophes in any of the written fields and remove them, as this version does not yet accept them.

How your data is used

Your questions are completely anonymous. None of your details will be shared with any third party.
